💡 English Learning Tips

🎧
Listen actively
Watch shows without subtitles. Then with English subtitles. Your ears adapt fast.
📝
Use words in sentences
Don't just memorize definitions. Write 3 sentences using a new word every day.
🔄
Spaced Repetition
Review words after 1 day, 3 days, 7 days, 1 month. This builds long-term memory.
🗣️
Talk to yourself
Narrate your day in English. Describe what you see. Speaking alone removes fear.
📱
Change phone language
Switch your device to English. Every notification becomes a mini-lesson.
🎬
Movies = real English
Movies teach slang, rhythm, emotion, and context that textbooks never capture.
📖
Read anything
Reddit, tweets, news, novels — all count. Quantity builds reading speed and vocabulary.
🏆
Track your progress
Mark words as learned, keep a vocabulary journal, celebrate small wins.
🌐
Think in English
Stop translating from Uzbek. Think directly in English. It feels slow at first — that's growth.
